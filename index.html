Smoked Meat Entrees

All of our meats are smoked low and slow on a wood-burning smoker. They are smoked with Oak, Hickory, Pecan, Cherry, and Peach woods enhancing the meat's natural flavor profiles.

Brisket

Succulent melt-in-your-mouth brisket smoked with oak for a deep rich flavor creating a beautiful bark sealing in all the juices.

Chicken

Moist and flavorful chicken marinated then smoked with a blend of cherry and pecan enhancing the meat's natural sweetness and creating a golden-brown exterior.

Pulled Pork

Tender and juicy pulled pork smoked with a blend of peach and hickory with rich marbling and a smoky flavor that is great for sandwiches, tacos, or all by itself.

Ribs

Meaty flavor-packed ribs that are hickory smoked to savory fall-off-the-bone perfection.
