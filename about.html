Welcome to Papa Clarke's Smokehouse

Our journey began with smoking food for special occasions with friends and family which led to my friends asking me to smoke meat for their own special occasions. That led to their friends and family wanting me to smoke food for them. Word of mouth started Papa Clarke's Smokehouse.

Our mission is to provide delicious smoked meats with exceptional flavors, we have perfected our recipes and smoking techniques to ensure every bite is savory. We select the finest cuts of meat and use only premium wood chips for smoking which creates unique flavors that increase the natural flavors of the meat.

We invite you to check out our menu and call or email to order for your next event!
